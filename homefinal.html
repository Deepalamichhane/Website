Renewable Energy & Climate Action

Obaku's owners invest in Obton solar parks, thereby offsetting enough CO2 yearly to cover all shipping of Obaku watches many times over - including your next Obaku order.

Quality Education

Through Obaku's Watches for Life program, we have supported the higher education of several young individuals with no other means and give regular support to a children's home in Thailand.

Responsible Production

Obaku supports the Recycle, Reduce and Reuse program. We seek to have a low carbon footprint and carefully control our waste, not only at our offices but also at our manufacturing facilities.
